About me



I am a UX Researcher and Software Developer based in Sydney, Australia. I have a passion for augmented reality, virtual reality, digital public displays, video games, and simple interfaces.

I hold a Bachelor of Computing, majoring in Human Interface Technology. The major emphasised cutting edge technologies, creativity and how to market ideas to the world. During this degree I was exposed to and gained interest in Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality technologies through a variety of projects undertaken together with the Human Interface Technology Laboratory Australia (HITLab AU).

Currently, I am working towards a PhD at the Design Lab in the University of Sydney. My topic explores how public displays can be personalised with technologies such as augmented reality.
